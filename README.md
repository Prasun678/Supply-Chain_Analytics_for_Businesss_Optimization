# Supply-Chain_Analytics_for_Businesss_Optimization
Analyzed Supply Chain Inefficiencies: Identified critical issues, including supplier disruptions, ineffective inventory management, and high late shipment rates (averaging 40%) across key product departments.

Inventory Insights: Highlighted inventory fluctuations due to overstocking and understocking, despite stable customer demand, leading to operational inefficiencies.

Business Intelligence Findings: Revealed that declining sales in legacy departments (e.g., Apparel, Footwear) stemmed from supply chain disruptions rather than changing customer preferences.

Proposed Data-Driven Solutions: Recommended optimizing supplier networks, focusing on growing markets (Asia), and reviving leading departments to meet evolving customer demands.

Supported Strategic Decisions: Provided actionable insights to improve inventory stability, enhance shipment efficiency, and align supply chain strategy with business goals.
